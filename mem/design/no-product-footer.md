---
name: No footer on product pages
description: Product/dashboard surfaces have no page-level footer; footers are reserved for SEO/content pages (matches OmenX SeoPageLayout convention).
type: constraint
---
Product / dashboard surfaces (home, event, trade, fans zone, positions, settings) MUST NOT have a page-level footer. Bottom padding is the visual end-of-page.

Footers are reserved for SEO / content / marketing pages (Insights, About, FAQ, Methodology, Privacy, Glossary, landings), matching OmenX's `SeoPageLayout` convention.

`Terms` / `Help` / cross-site OmenX links go in the top-right user menu (`FanZoneHeader` menu), not a bottom bar.

**Why:** Product pages are tools, not destinations. A footer adds vertical weight and pulls users out of the workflow. The OmenX app pages (events trading, positions, dashboards, settings) all follow this rule.
